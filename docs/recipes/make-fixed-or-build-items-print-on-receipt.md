## How do I make fixed items or build items print on a receipt?

Usage Examples:
* I want to ensure the kitchen always includes a roll with an entree
* I want to make sure the kitchen has a list of all of the ingredients for a menu item, such as lettuce, mayo, pickles, etc.
* I have bundles items that do not change that I want to ensure are all listed on the receipt
* I want to remind a new bartender what ingredients go in unique, signature drinks 

In the menu item, go to the build items page. For every item you always want printed on the receipt, enable the "Include on receipt" button.
